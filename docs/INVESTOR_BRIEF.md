**Confidential | March 2026**

## A. ONE-LINER

Terravault lets anyone invest in premium Latin American real estate starting at $100 through compliant, blockchain-based fractional ownership.

---

## B. PROBLEM (With Data)

Latin America sits on **$3.2 trillion** in real estate value, yet the asset class remains fundamentally broken for investors:

| Pain Point | Data |
|---|---|
| Institutional properties inaccessible to retail investors | **92%** of institutional-grade LATAM real estate is off-limits to non-HNWI (Inter-American Development Bank, 2024) |
| Prohibitive transaction costs | Settlement costs consume **8-12%** of transaction value -- legal fees, notary, transfer taxes, intermediaries |
| Glacial settlement times | **60-120 days** average close time vs. seconds for digital assets |
| Capital lock-up | No liquid secondary market; average hold period **7-10 years** with no exit optionality |
| Cross-border friction | Foreign buyers face layered bureaucracy -- foreign ownership restrictions, currency controls, opaque title registries |
| Demographic exclusion | **67% of LATAM's population** is under 40 and digitally native, yet locked out of real estate wealth creation (ECLAC, 2024) |

**The core insight:** The world's fastest-growing investor demographic (young, mobile-first, globally connected) has zero access to the world's most proven wealth-building asset class in their own region.

**Market failure cost:** An estimated **$500B+ in capital** sits on the sidelines because minimum investment thresholds, regulatory complexity, and illiquidity make LATAM real estate inaccessible at scale.

---

## C. SOLUTION

Terravault tokenizes institutional-grade LATAM real estate into **compliant, tradeable digital securities** using the ERC-3643 (T-REX) standard on Polygon -- the only security token standard with built-in identity verification and transfer restrictions at the protocol level.

**10x improvements over the status quo:**

| Dimension | Traditional | Terravault |
|---|---|---|
| Minimum investment | $50,000 - $500,000 | **$100** |
| Settlement time | 60-120 days | **< 30 seconds** |
| Transaction cost | 8-12% | **< 1%** |
| Liquidity | Locked 7-10 years | **24/7 secondary trading** |
| Geographic access | Local only | **Global, permissioned** |
| Yield distribution | Quarterly checks, manual | **Automated, on-chain, real-time** |
| Transparency | Opaque SPV structures | **Fully auditable on-chain** |
| Compliance | Manual, jurisdiction-dependent | **Programmable, embedded in token** |

**Key differentiators:**
- **ERC-3643 compliance**: Identity verification, transfer restrictions, and regulatory rules are encoded directly in the token -- not bolted on as an afterthought
- **Chainlink oracle integration**: Real-time property valuations from independent appraisals feed on-chain, eliminating stale NAV pricing
- **Automated dividend distribution**: Rental yields flow directly to token holder wallets via smart contract -- no manual processing
- **ONCHAINID (ERC-735/725)**: Portable, reusable identity credentials for investors across multiple properties

## D. WHY NOW

1. **Regulatory tailwinds**: Colombia (Law 2069/2020), Mexico (Fintech Law 2018, secondary regulations 2024), Brazil (CVM Resolution 88/2022), and Chile (Fintech Law 2023) have all created legal frameworks for digital securities and tokenized assets in the past 3 years. The regulatory window is open.

2. **ERC-3643 maturity**: The T-REX standard reached production readiness in 2023-2024 with institutional adoption by Tokeny, Securitize, and Archax. The compliance tooling is now battle-tested.

3. **Polygon ecosystem**: Polygon PoS offers sub-cent transaction costs, 2-second finality, and the largest DeFi/RWA ecosystem on any EVM L2. Franklin Templeton, BlackRock (via Securitize), and Ondo Finance have all deployed RWA products on Polygon.

4. **RWA institutional momentum**: BlackRock's BUIDL fund crossed $500M in 2024. Total on-chain RWA TVL exceeded $12B by early 2025. The institutional narrative has shifted from "if" to "how fast."

5. **LATAM digital adoption**: 75% smartphone penetration, 400M+ internet users, and the region's crypto adoption ranks #2 globally (Chainalysis, 2024). The user base is ready.

6. **Demographic pressure**: 200M+ LATAM millennials and Gen Z are excluded from real estate. They are comfortable with digital wallets, crypto, and mobile-first financial products.

## M. RISKS AND MITIGATIONS

| # | Risk | Severity | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Regulatory change** -- LATAM governments reverse or restrict digital securities frameworks | High | Multi-jurisdictional approach diversifies risk; active engagement with regulators; legal counsel in each market; SPV structures designed for regulatory flexibility |
| 2 | **Liquidity cold-start** -- insufficient secondary market trading volume makes tokens illiquid | High | Market-making partnerships; yield-bearing tokens provide returns even without trading; liquidity mining incentives in early phase; focus on supply quality to drive organic demand |
| 3 | **Property risk** -- vacancy, tenant default, natural disaster, or property devaluation | Medium | Conservative LTV (<65%); institutional-grade properties only; insurance requirements; diversified portfolio across geographies and property types; independent appraisals via Chainlink oracle |
| 4 | **Smart contract risk** -- exploit or bug in ERC-3643 token or dividend contracts | Medium | Formal audit by Trail of Bits or OpenZeppelin; bug bounty program; ERC-3643 is battle-tested (200+ deployments); phased rollout with caps; insurance via Nexus Mutual |
| 5 | **Adoption risk** -- crypto UX friction deters mainstream investors | Medium | Fiat on-ramp integration; custodial wallet option for non-crypto users; progressive decentralization; mobile-first UX with no blockchain jargon in investor-facing UI |
