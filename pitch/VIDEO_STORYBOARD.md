# TERRAVAULT — VIDEO STORYBOARD
## 60-90 Second Pitch Video
### RWA Demo Day — Hong Kong Web3 Festival

---

## TONE

Cinematic. Confident. Inevitable. Think Apple launch film meets Bloomberg documentary. No hype words. No "revolutionize." No "Web3 buzzword salad." Just a clear, visual, emotionally honest story about unlocking something that should have been unlocked a long time ago.

**Music:** Minimal electronic. Slow build. Think Hans Zimmer meets Tycho. Starts ambient, ends with a pulse.

**Color palette:** Deep blacks, warm golds, clean whites. LATAM warmth meets institutional trust.

**Aspect ratio:** 16:9 (primary), 9:16 cut for socials.

## PRODUCTION NOTES

### Video Style References
- **Stripe's "The New Standard"** — clean product shots, institutional confidence
- **Apple "Introducing iPhone"** — the pause, the reveal, the simplicity
- **BlackRock's RWA institutional videos** — credibility through understatement

### Voiceover
- One voice. Male or female. Calm. Authoritative. Not a pitch voice — a storytelling voice. Think: someone explaining something inevitable, not selling something uncertain.
- Record in a treated room. No reverb. Intimate and close.

### Footage Options
- **Ideal:** 1-2 days of original drone + street footage in Bogota or Sao Paulo
- **Budget alternative:** Licensed stock footage (Artgrid, Shutterstock) of LATAM cities, property, people. Can be very effective if color-graded to match.
- **Platform footage:** Screen-recorded at 4K from a staging environment, then composited into device frames with After Effects or similar.

### Editing Rhythm
- Cuts on the beat. Every transition should feel intentional.
- Text appears word by word or line by line. Never all at once.
- The "glass shattering" moment in Scene 3 is the emotional pivot. Everything before it is tension. Everything after it is release.

### Length Targets
- **Ideal cut:** 75 seconds
- **Short cut (socials):** 30 seconds (Scenes 1, 3, 4.2, 7)
- **Extended cut (website):** 2 minutes (add team, traction, market data)

### Deliverables
- 16:9 master (4K)
- 9:16 social cut (1080x1920)
- 1:1 square cut for LinkedIn/Twitter
- Thumbnail still (Scene 3, post-shatter, logo reveal)
- Subtitles in English, Spanish, Portuguese, Mandarin
